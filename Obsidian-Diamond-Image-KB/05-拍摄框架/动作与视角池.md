# 动作与视角池

动作：
- holding coffee
- opening a cafe door
- adjusting hair
- checking phone
- taking mirror selfie
- stepping out of a car
- holding a clutch
- holding phone naturally
- holding sunglasses
- looking away mid-step
- talking to a friend
- laughing mid-conversation
- checking lipstick in mirror
- leaning on a bar table
- fixing sleeve
- sitting by a window
- looking away from camera

场景-动作兼容规则：
- 餐厅 / 咖啡店 / 私人晚宴：sitting across the table, reaching for a glass or menu, leaning into conversation, laughing mid-conversation, holding phone naturally
- 豪车内 / 私人交通：settling into a seat, getting out of a car, holding phone naturally, looking out of window, adjusting coat while seated
- 酒店大堂 / 电梯镜面 / 商场走廊：crossing a lobby, walking through doorway, holding a clutch, checking outfit in mirror, turning body while walking
- 试衣间 / 精品店 dressing area：checking outfit in mirror, trying on outerwear, fixing sleeve, holding phone naturally, taking mirror selfie
- 艺术馆 / 博物馆 / 画廊开幕：browsing art, turning toward a friend, holding sunglasses or small clutch, looking away from camera, stepping into light
- 婚礼 / bridal shower / 花园活动：walking through doorway, laughing mid-conversation, turning toward a friend, holding a clutch, looking away with a relaxed smile
- 度假阳台 / 游艇会 / 屋顶酒廊：stepping into light, sitting by a window, holding sunglasses or small clutch, leaning slightly into conversation, looking away mid-step
- 普拉提 / wellness club / 高端运动场景：checking phone, adjusting hair, checking outfit in mirror, fixing sleeve, holding water or towel naturally
- fashion week arrival / 街拍：walking past camera, looking away mid-step, holding sunglasses, checking phone, talking to a friend
- afterparty / 酒吧 / direct-flash 场景：leaning on a bar table, laughing mid-conversation, holding a clutch, turning toward flash, checking lipstick in mirror
- backstage mirror / 时装后台：checking lipstick in mirror, adjusting hair, fixing dress strap, holding phone naturally, taking mirror selfie

抽取要求：
- 先选场景，再从该场景兼容动作里选动作。
- 如果动作和场景不兼容，必须重抽，不允许进入生图。
- 动作只给方向，不写死完整剧本。
- 动作要自然大方，不要刻意展示产品；只有产品特写必须明确展示产品。
- 全身图和半身图禁止为了展示产品而摸戒指、摸项链、把手伸向镜头。
- `touching necklace`、`resting hand near collarbone`、`hand close to camera` 只能作为低频特写动作，不作为全身/半身默认动作。

产品特写动作框架：
- 钻戒：hand beside a wine glass, hand holding a phone, hand on car door handle, hand holding a clutch, hand resting on restaurant table, fingers near makeup compact
- 项链：necklace visible during a natural head turn, collarbone crop while turning toward a friend, necklace resting on skin above dress neckline, close crop with hair and neckline context
- 耳饰：earrings visible through hair, profile crop during a head turn, hand lightly moving hair away from ear, earlobe close crop with real hair texture
- 手链：bracelet near sleeve edge, wrist near steering wheel, wrist holding phone, hand resting on table edge, bracelet beside a watch without stealing product focus

特写抽取规则：
- 特写动作必须和产品品类匹配。
- 戒指特写优先手部生活化动作。
- 项链特写优先锁骨、领口、转头动作。
- 耳饰特写优先侧脸、耳垂、头发遮挡关系。
- 手链特写优先手腕、袖口、桌面或车内局部。
- 特写可以有生活场景残留，但产品必须清楚。

视角：
- phone photo from a friend
- mirror selfie
- across-the-table shot
- street candid
- close crop
- standing-height camera
- natural hand-held angle
- slight motion
- direct-flash social photo
- fashion week candid crop
- off-duty model street crop
- close lifestyle crop

避免：
- 傻站
- 正面硬摆拍
- 明星红毯站姿重复
- 手部刻意展示到不自然
- 过度商业广告动作
- 场景和动作不匹配，例如餐厅试衣镜、车内走路、运动房拿晚宴酒杯
- 全身和半身把产品当主角
- 僵硬棚拍产品特写
