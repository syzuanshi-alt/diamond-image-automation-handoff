# 场景变量池

原则：真实、欧美、生活化但有消费力。

场景：
- upscale cafe
- high-end restaurant
- restaurant direct-flash snapshot
- outdoor wedding
- hotel lobby lounge
- hotel hallway flash photo
- fitting room mirror selfie
- car back seat selfie
- car arrival paparazzi-style
- city sidewalk outside a restaurant
- editorial street-style sidewalk
- boutique dressing area
- boutique changing area
- rooftop dinner
- rooftop party
- art gallery opening
- gallery opening
- museum courtyard
- home closet natural light
- backstage mirror
- fashion week arrival
- afterparty flash photo
- airport lounge
- private dinner
- luxury department store fitting room
- townhouse entrance
- wine bar
- bridal shower

氛围：
- real candid
- social media photo
- camera roll style
- fashion week candid
- off-duty model candid
- 90s-coded editorial
- afterparty direct flash
- magazine-adjacent but unstaged
- friend-taken photo
- mirror selfie
- soft natural daylight
- cloudy afternoon
- evening indoor light
- normal background clutter
- casually wealthy
- understated luxury

避免：
- 过度豪华模板场景
- 永远金色夕阳
- 过度虚化背景
- 像广告棚拍
- 空无一人的假场景
- 每张都在酒店大堂
- 每张都像成熟外套贵妇
- 每张都像棚拍时尚大片

抽取规则：
- 每套只选 1 个主场景。
- 可以附加 1 个氛围词。
- 场景不要描述到固定城市、固定餐厅、固定布景。
- 场景选定后，动作必须从兼容动作池抽取。
- 如果要增加新场景，必须同时补充该场景的兼容动作类型。
- 年轻网红和时装模特方向优先使用 fashion week arrival、afterparty flash、street-style sidewalk、backstage mirror、boutique changing area、rooftop party 等场景。
